TaskCountDown helps you stay on top of deadlines by showing exactly how many days remain.

Add your tasks and deadlines, and the app counts down the days automatically — so you always know what's coming up and what needs your attention now.

[Features]

■ Deadline Countdown
See the number of days remaining for each task at a glance. Overdue, today, soon, and future tasks are color-coded for quick scanning.

■ Categories
Organize tasks by category — Work, Personal, Study, Health, and more — each with its own icon and color.

■ Memo
Attach a note to any task to keep relevant details close at hand.

■ Push Notifications
Get reminded before your deadline arrives. Set notifications per task or globally.

■ Completion Tracking
Mark tasks as done and see how many days elapsed since the deadline.

■ Multilingual
Supports English, Japanese, Thai, Vietnamese, and Chinese (Simplified).

No ads. No unnecessary features. Just your deadlines, clearly counted down.
